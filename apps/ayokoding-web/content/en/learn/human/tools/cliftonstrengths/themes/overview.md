The CliftonStrengths assessment identifies your top talent themes from 34 possible themes, organized into four leadership domains. Understanding these themes helps you leverage your natural talents and work more effectively.

## Four Leadership Domains

- **Executing** - Themes focused on making things happen and getting work done
- **Influencing** - Themes focused on taking charge, speaking up, and ensuring your voice is heard
- **Relationship Building** - Themes focused on connecting with others and holding teams together
- **Strategic Thinking** - Themes focused on absorbing and analyzing information to make better decisions

Explore each domain to understand how your themes work together.
